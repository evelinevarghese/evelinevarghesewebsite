Our Menu

Homemade apple pie

Indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of our homemade apple pie. Made with a flaky, buttery crust and filled with a generous amount of juicy, cinnamon-spiced apples, this classic dessert is the perfect balance of sweetness and tartness. Served with a dollop of freshly whipped cream or a scoop of creamy vanilla ice cream, our apple pie is sure to delight your taste buds and evoke feelings of nostalgia. Treat yourself to a slice of this timeless favorite and experience the heartwarming taste of homemade goodness.

Mediterranean Lamb Burger

Experience a mouthwatering twist on the classic burger with our Mediterranean Lamb Burger. Made with succulent, handcrafted lamb patties seasoned with aromatic herbs and spices, this burger offers a delightful fusion of flavors. Topped with tangy feta cheese, crisp lettuce, juicy tomato slices, and a dollop of refreshing tzatziki sauce, every bite is a burst of Mediterranean goodness. Served on a toasted brioche bun and accompanied by a side of perfectly seasoned fries, our Mediterranean Lamb Burger is a culinary delight that will transport you to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean.

Mediterranean Veggie Sandwich

Indulge in a vibrant explosion of flavors with our Mediterranean Veggie Sandwich. Packed with fresh, locally sourced vegetables, this wholesome delight is a vegetarian's dream come true. Savor the delightful combination of roasted red peppers, sun-dried tomatoes, tangy feta cheese, crisp cucumbers, and creamy hummus, all layered between slices of freshly baked artisanal bread. The medley of textures and the harmonious blend of Mediterranean spices will transport you to the sunny coasts of Greece. Served with a side of mixed greens or crispy fries, our Mediterranean Veggie Sandwich is a satisfying and healthy choice for any time of the day.

Cinnamon Sugar Donut Delight

Indulge in the sweet and irresistible pleasure of our Cinnamon Sugar Donut Delight. This delectable treat features a fluffy, freshly fried donut generously coated in a heavenly blend of cinnamon and sugar. As you take a bite, you'll experience the warm, melt-in-your-mouth sensation of the perfectly balanced flavors. The delicate outer layer gives way to a soft, pillowy interior that's bound to satisfy your cravings. Pair it with a steaming cup of freshly brewed coffee or a creamy latte for the ultimate indulgence. Whether enjoyed as a breakfast treat or a delightful dessert, our Cinnamon Sugar Donut Delight is a classic favorite that will leave you craving for more.
